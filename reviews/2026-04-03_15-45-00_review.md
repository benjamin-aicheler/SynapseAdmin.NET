## 🚨 1. Security & Vulnerabilities

*Focus: Hardcoded secrets, injection risks (SQL, XSS, Command), path traversal, insecure dependencies, authorization bypass.*

**Issues Found:**
* **URL Injection Risk (Low/Medium):** In `UserService.GetUserListAsync` (line 25) and `RoomService.GetRoomListAsync` (line 25), the `orderBy` parameter is appended directly to the URL without validation or escaping. Although currently `MudTh` components do not define `SortLabel`, adding them in the future would allow users to inject arbitrary URL parameters.

**Recommended Fixes:**

* Use a whitelist for `SortLabel` values in the services.
